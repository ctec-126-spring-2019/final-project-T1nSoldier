Welcome to Super Dice!

Rules

Click Roll the Dice.

Each dice roll costs 5 coins. Each roll is 4 dice.

Your dice roll will be displayed. You will then wager whether the next dice roll will be higher or lower by pressing the corresponding button.

If you are correct, your earnings multiplier will double, and you will have to wager on the next dice roll being higher or lower. The multiplier caps at 32 coins.

The game continues until you guess incorrectly. Your multiplier will be reset when you lose.

Choose your dice pattern. This may be changed at any time.
